Gear Up For War

Master every aspect of survival in a fast-paced low-poly world

Third-Person Combat

Tight TPS gunplay with ADS, recoil patterns, bullet tracers, and 13 unique weapons. Shoulder-swap, screen shake, and hitscan with server-authoritative validation.

Base Building

Gather resources and build multi-story bases with foundations, walls, doors, stairs, and roofs. Snap-to-grid placement with tool cupboard protection.

Loot & Craft

Scavenge ground loot and death bags. Craft weapons, tools, medical supplies, and explosives from gathered resources. Tiered loot tables with weighted rarity.

Closing Zone

5-phase shrinking danger zone forces players into tighter confrontations. Take damage outside the safe area. Strategic positioning is key to victory.

Choose Your Mode

Two distinct ways to play, each with its own strategies and stakes

Survival

Open-world PvPvE sandbox. Build your base, farm resources, defend against raids, and dominate the map.

  • Open world with terrain generation
  • Base building with snap-to-grid
  • Resource gathering and crafting
  • PvP and PvE encounters
  • Persistent bases and inventory

Battle Royale

30 players drop into a generated city. Loot weapons, fight to survive, and outlast everyone as the zone closes in.

  • 30-player matches
  • Parachute drop from flight path
  • Procedural city map
  • 5-phase shrinking zone
  • Ground loot and death bags
